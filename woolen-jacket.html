Natural Colors with Fleece Lining Handmade in Nepal from sheep's wool

Woolen handmade is one of the leading industry in Nepal. Small family factory produce all types of wool items like hippie Handmade Wool Jackets, Handmade Knit Wool Socks, Handmade Wool Ear Flaps, Handmade Wool Animal Hats, Handmade Wool Glove, Handmade Wool Hairband, Hippy Wool Knitted Jumpers and Sweater, Handmade Wool Poncho, Handmade Wool Warmer, Wool Slipper Socks. 





Woolen jacket has added a fleece lining for smooth comfort and extra warm to make your winter warmer meeting the standards of Fair Trade Principles.


We have five size for woolen jacket such as Small, Medium, Large, XL and XXL.


Our tailor and designer will be happy to work on a new design.

If you have an idea for a new pattern or a new design for a jacket, then you can send us a request to create your own individual design, according to your requirements. This will be a joint work, as a result of which you will receive your unique product.




Warm women's cardigan with patchwork pattern with various natural color surfaces including light brown, anthracite and beige.

The vest has a fixed hood and is fully lined with fleece, both on the body, the arms, the hood and in the pockets.

This warm cardigan has side pockets and a lockable inner pocket and the zippers strong quality. This warm item can be worn as a jacket in the winter but is also very comfortable on cool summer days. Available in all sizes S to XXL.
